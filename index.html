About CHRISM Associates

CHRISM Associates is a professional corporate finance and advisory firm dedicated to driving sustainable financial growth and empowering strategic decision-making for businesses across diverse industries. We provide tailored solutions in corporate finance, M&A, risk management, investment, compliance, and AI-driven analytics.
